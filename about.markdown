Hi! I'm Revanth Kolli.

# I'm a writer. 

But I'm mostly just paid to write code, documentation and the odd, yet inevitable postmortem (the software kind). 

# Okay fine, I'm really a software engineer.

But I love stories as much as I love developing software. Whether it’s a fascinating thriller film, murder mystery series or engrossing historical fiction novel, I love learning about the why behind our choices and actions. 

>Stories are the best medium for sharing knowledge and by learning more about each other, we can make the world a bit better for everyone.

# So why this blog?

Occasionally, I explore what I'm learning by writing reviews, stories, Telugu* songs and other posts. Writing helps me refine my own thoughts and views about the world. 

Since learning and exploration is the focus of this blog, I won't be taking an audience into consideration. This means that my posts can vary in quality, clarity and breadth of topic. If you find anything I write useful, that's great! Just don't go expecting that to happen routinely because that's not the objective.


# The rest

I'm currently a software engineer at Yext in NYC helping global companies take control of their digital brand experience.

I was previously at UVA, Capital One, Center for Open Science and SWIFT.

When I'm not writing or consuming code or stories, I'm usually playing games with family and friends and learning how to cook new dishes.

\* Telugu is a South Indian language spoken in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana
