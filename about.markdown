# I'm a writer. 

But I'm mostly just paid to write code, documentation and the odd, yet inevitable postmortem (the software kind). 

Yes, I am aware they call that a software engineer. 

# Okay fine, I'm really a software engineer.

But I also love stories. Whether it's a fascinating thriller film, murder mystery series or engrossing historical fiction novel, I love learning about the why behind what keeps the world spinning. I believe stories are the best medium for sharing knowledge and by learning more about each other, we can make the world a little bit better for everyone.

# So why this blog?

Occasionally, I refine what I'm learning into reviews, stories, Telugu* songs and other posts. I don't know if that's useful for anyone else, but it helps me refine my thoughts and view of the world. Anything else that comes out of it is just a bonus.

# The rest

I'm currently in NYC. When I'm not writing or consuming stories, I'm usually playing games with family and friends or learning how to cook new dishes.

\* Telugu is a South Indian language
